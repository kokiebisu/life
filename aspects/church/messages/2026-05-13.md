## ポイント

1. **ジーザスを学ばずに神を知ることはできない** — You can't know God without learning Jesus. ジーザス抜きで神を定義すると、道を間違える
2. **ジーザスは聖書を通して現される** — 人を通してではなく、聖書を通して神を知る。人に騙されてはいけない
3. **常にキリストと自分を比較する** — Always compare yourself with Christ.

---

## メモ

### How can you know God?

- You can learn about Jesus through the word of God (Bible)
- Not by man. Let God be revealed by the scripture. Don't be deceived by man.
- Even Nazis believed they were doing the same. They thought they were right.
- **If we have a wrong view of God, it can turn us into monsters.**

### Why should we do devotions?

- So we don't get deceived on Jesus' image
- Do devotions → Jesus will be revealed by the scriptures
- **Ask Jesus to reveal himself to you. He wants to.** (John 7:16)
- Be sure to keep an open heart

### Jesus's role in your life

- In times, Jesus will convict you, challenge you — but at the end applauds you (John 14:21)
- **You will not be punished for your sin, but punished by your sin.**
- **Greatest sin is not your sin, but unresolved sin.**
- The more you get to know Jesus, the more you get to know yourself — You were created by the image of God

### Responding to revelation

- When Jesus reveals to you, you need to respond before he reveals more
- マタイ 21:27 — すでに知っている真理に対して責任を持てないなら、なぜこれ以上真理を与えようか？

### Measuring yourself

- 第一コリント 10:12 — When you compare yourself with someone more evil, you feel like you're a good person. **You have to measure yourself with Christ.**
- Always compare yourself with Christ
- Distance between Nazis vs Distance with Christ — **We are not so different from the Nazis.**
