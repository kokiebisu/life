## Main Points

### The Four Foundations of Growth (成長の4つのカテゴリー)

1. **家柄・出自** - Family background, origins
2. **地位・評判** - Status, reputation
3. **道徳的生活態度** - Moral lifestyle
4. **成果・成功** - Achievements, success

We can stand on any combination of these, as Paul once did. But these are not solid ground.

### Rules vs. Transformation

- Rules are not the path to change; they are like shields
- Rules lead us to judge others
- Paul became a catalyst for a new energy — an explosive, worldwide movement
- **Love is what leads to growth in all things**

### Getting More by Letting Go (手に入れるとは手放すこと)

This is healthy growth. If we want to stand on the foundation of Christ's love, we must step down from these four foundations.

These foundations are not evil — they are **garbage (ゴミ)**. They shake and crumble:
- If you live for success, you will fail
- If you live for fame, a single rumor can bring you down

Paul threw away his foundations of success at the foot of the cross:
- His Jewish heritage
- His Benjamin tribe lineage
- His titles and credentials

All laid down before the cross.

## Dealing with Anxiety (思い煩いと向き合う)

**"Do not be anxious about anything"**

Paul knew the coldness of fear firsthand. When he said "do not be anxious," he was not telling us to suppress our emotions. He was telling us to:
- Accept the anxiety
- Turn and redirect it toward God

> 「どんなことでも、思い煩うのはやめなさい。何事につけ、感謝を込めて祈りと願いをささげ、求めているものを神に打ち明けなさい。そうすれば、あらゆる人知を超える神の平和が、あなたがたの心と考えとをキリスト・イエスによって守るでしょう。」
