# Sermons

A collection space for sermon notes, teachings, and spiritual messages.

## Purpose

- Capture key insights from sermons and teachings
- Build a personal library of spiritual wisdom
- Track themes and lessons over time
- Support continued reflection and application

## Guidance

### Taking Notes
- Record the date, speaker, and Scripture reference
- Capture main points and key messages
- Note personal convictions or action items
- Write down memorable quotes or illustrations

### Reflection Process
- Review notes within 24 hours to reinforce learning
- Identify one actionable takeaway to implement
- Share insights with others to deepen understanding
- Revisit past sermons for continued growth

## Structure

Organize sermon notes by:
- Date
- Speaker or pastor
- Scripture passage
- Theme or series title
